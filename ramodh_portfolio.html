Who I Am

Where water
meets science

I am a DAAD-funded PhD candidate at the Department of Hydrology and Water Management, Institute for Nature and Resource Conservation, Faculty of Agricultural and Nutritional Sciences at Christian-Albrechts-University of Kiel.

My doctoral thesis — "Assessment and Optimization of Selected Ecological Methods for Agricultural Water Treatment" — explores nature-based solutions including reactive ditches and quantifying ecological consequences of vegetation change in the Kielstau Catchment.

Before moving to Germany, I served as an Environmental Scientist at Sri Lanka's National Aquatic Resources Research and Development Agency (NARA) for four years, leading research in coastal water quality, marine conservation, and treatment technology.
